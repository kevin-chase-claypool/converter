Current Operating Assumptions

  • Motion controller: grblHAL on RP23CNC owns parsing, planning, homing, limits, and step generation.
  • Axes: X/Y/A are used. Z may appear in the firmware builder only to expose A, but Z is unused and unwired.
  • A units: converter A values are motor-shaft degrees. One bed revolution is 4320 A motor degrees with the 12:1 ratio.
  • Toolhead: M3 means ENGAGE and M5 means LIFT through a spindle/tool output state.
  • Normal homing: ioSender sends M5, waits for lift dwell, then sends $H. grblHAL homes X/Y switches and A from validated RP2040 A_HOME.
  • Magnetic setup: center scan and two-revolution A scan are setup/calibration flows, not manual daily coordination steps.

Open Blockers

  • RP23CNC input polarity and electrical requirements are not yet verified.
  • RP2040 A_HOME output-driver behavior is not yet proven against the selected RP23CNC input.
  • TMAG5273 thresholds, hysteresis, offset, and repeatability are still TBD.
  • TB6600 input topology, switch settings, and safe current limits need bench evidence.
  • Toolhead controller placement and fault wiring remain pending.

Recently Clarified

  • TMAG5273 is fixed-height on the toolhead/gantry; it is not a Z sensor.
  • A-axis magnetic homing uses exactly two full bed revolutions during the bounds scan.
  • The pen must be lifted before homing, calibration scans, and travel moves.
  • Abort paths must stop motion, keep the pen lifted, preserve diagnostics, and require explicit recovery.
